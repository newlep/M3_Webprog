Mapua Malayan Colleges Mindanao Azure Pantheons

About us

Founded in 2022, MMCM Azure Pantheons is more than just an organization. It showcases the unwavering spirit of inspiration and the undying passion for esport. Established by student leaders who formerly led the Computer Student Society, this group is dedicated to enhancing the gaming experience to previously unheard-of levels.
